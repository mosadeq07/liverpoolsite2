Club History

Liverpool Football Club was founded in 1892 and quickly grew into one of Englands most successful and iconic teams. Under legendary managers like Bill Shankly and Bob Paisley, the club built a winning culture based on passion, hard work, and the famous “Youll Never Walk Alone” spirit. Throughout the 1970s and 1980s, Liverpool dominated English and European football, winning multiple league titles and European Cups. The club has continued to compete at the highest level in modern times, adding major trophies like the 2019 Champions League and the 2020 Premier League. Today, Liverpool is known not just for its success, but for its loyal supporters, rich traditions, and deep connection to the city itself.
